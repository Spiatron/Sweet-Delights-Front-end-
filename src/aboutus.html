Berif History

Sweet Delight is the proud sister company of the iconic Bombay Bakery of Hyderabad, known for its legacy of exquisite baking. Established to blend traditional flavors with modern twists, Sweet Delight continues the family tradition of excellence. Our journey began with the vision to preserve the rich heritage of Bombay Bakery while introducing innovative and contemporary creations. Over the years, we have grown into a beloved destination for dessert lovers, maintaining the same dedication to quality and taste that made our predecessor a household name.
